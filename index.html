Notes

Temperature

Minimum internal temperature 145°F

Serving Size

Divide equally, 10 ounces (285 grams)

Yield

10 servings

Techniques

Deep-Frying

Geographic Region

Japan

Tempura-Fried Seafood and Vegetable Medley

Ingredients

Main Dish

  • 20 pieces Snapper fillets, boneless, sliced into thin strips (3 pounds/1.4 kilograms)
  • 20 pieces Shrimp, 16/20, peeled, deveined (1 ¼ pounds/566 grams)
  • 30 pieces Squid, cleaned, cut into strips (2 pounds/905 grams)
  • 2 pounds Shiitake mushrooms, cleaned, trimmed
  • 2 pounds Sweet potatoes, washed, peeled, washed, cut into batonnette
  • 1 to 2 each Onion, peeled, sliced into rings
  • 1 to 2 each Carrots, washed, peeled, washed, cut thin on bias
  • Flour, all-purpose, for dipping, As needed

Batter

  • 8 ounces Flour
  • 2 teaspoons Baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 2 each Eggs, beaten
  • 6 ounces Milk
  • 8 ounces Dashi
  • ⅓ cup or 3 ounces Mirin
  • ⅓ cup or 3 ounces Soy sauce
  • 1 to 2 teaspoons Ginger, fresh, grated

Preparation

  1. Gather all the ingredients and equipment.
  2. repare the batter by first sifting the dry ingredients into a bowl. Combine eggs, milk and oil and slowly pour into the dry ingredients while whisking until batter is smooth. Refrigerate until needed.
  3. Combine the dipping sauce ingredients in a small saucepan. Heat to a boil, remove from heat and keep warm for service.
  4. Prepare the main ingredients for the tempura. Carrots and sweet potatoes should be blanched lightly in boiling salted water or by steaming. Shock, drain, and reserve.
  5. Ready the fry station by preheating the oil to 350°F (177°C), assembling all main ingredients in a hotel pan (on ice), and having the batter ready as well as all necessary utensils.
  6. Begin with the vegetables. First dredge lightly in flour, then dip into the tempura batter. Allow excess batter to drip off and carefully drop into the hot oil, working as quickly as possible. Fry all items until lightly golden and crisp. Remove with a skimmer or slotted spoon, place to drain and keep warm under a heat lamp and proceed on to the next items.
  7. Each portion should receive an ample assortment of fried vegetables, 2 shrimp, 2 fish pieces, and 3 strips of fried squid.
  8. Arrange on heated plates accompanied by a small cup of the dipping sauce. Garnish as desired.
